About

surewhynot.app is a small lab-style site built around simple, inspectable tools.

Sometimes you don’t need AI. You just need the right words.

The phrase generator runs entirely in your browser. No logins, no tracking, no data sent anywhere.

Tools may change. Experiments may disappear. That’s intentional.

If it’s useful — good. If it sparks an idea — even better.
