About
SureWhyNot is a small, simple site built for quick utility tools and lightweight games.

- Get something done (export, convert, etc.)
- Then chill for a minute (type race, mini puzzle)
No accounts. No “social feed.” No pressure. This is meant to be a calm corner that stays fast and minimal.
